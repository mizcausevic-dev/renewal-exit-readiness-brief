# Kinetic Gain Embedded

Renewal Exit Readiness Brief is designed to open an embedded executive-intelligence engagement:

- compare renewal language against delivery freshness, sponsor continuity, churn signals, and commercial exposure
- identify which account clusters should renew, save, escalate, or exit before the next board or investor review
- convert commercial drift into one usable remediation plan with explicit recovery and exit-sequencing decisions

This is an operator-facing renewal-intelligence surface, not legal advice, financial advice, or a live customer system.
